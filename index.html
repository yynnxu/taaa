Who We Are

Dedicated to fostering appreciation and understanding

The TAAA is dedicated to fostering a deep appreciation and understanding of Asian American culture and heritage while advocating for racial equality and justice.

Read our full mission →

Cultural Celebration

Celebrating and preserving the rich tapestry of Asian American cultural traditions, including art, cuisine, music, language, and customs.

Community Building

Through events, workshops, and initiatives, we highlight how Asian Americans contribute to Texas society and demonstrate cultural diversity.

Education & Advocacy

Educating the broader community about Asian American experiences, challenges, achievements, and combating stereotypes to promote inclusivity.
