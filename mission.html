Our Mission

Dedicated to fostering appreciation and understanding of Asian American culture and heritage

The Texas Asian American Association (TAAA) is dedicated to fostering a deep appreciation and understanding of Asian American culture and heritage while advocating for racial equality and justice.

Cultural Celebration

We work to celebrate and preserve the rich tapestry of Asian American cultural traditions, including art, cuisine, music, language, and customs. Our events and programs showcase the beauty and diversity of Asian American heritage across Texas.

Community Showcase

Through events, workshops, and initiatives, TAAA highlights how Asian Americans contribute to Texas society and demonstrates their cultural diversity. We create spaces for connection, collaboration, and mutual support within and beyond our community.

Education

The association educates the broader community about Asian American experiences, challenges, achievements, historical importance, and contemporary significance. We believe that education is the foundation for building understanding and respect.

Cross-Cultural Understanding

By fostering dialogue and understanding between communities, TAAA endeavors to combat stereotypes and promote inclusivity. We believe that a more connected and empathetic society benefits everyone.
